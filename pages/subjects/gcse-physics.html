How We Teach

A personalised approach that builds confidence, true understanding, and results that last.

1

1. Diagnostic first

I begin with the goals parents and students bring to me, whether it is passing an exam, improving confidence, or filling gaps. Everything I do is adapted to the student’s needs.

2

2. Guided teaching

When I notice missing knowledge, I go back and rebuild the foundations until the student truly understands. No skipping and no rushing, just clarity.

3

3. Targeted retrieval

Every student learns differently. I find out what motivates them, whether it is cars, football, or music, and use that to make lessons engaging. Physics becomes relevant, understandable, and easier to remember.

4

4. Perform under pressure

I know what it’s like to worry about being judged. My students never have to feel embarrassed about not knowing something. With patience and practice, I help them build confidence for exams and understanding that really lasts.
