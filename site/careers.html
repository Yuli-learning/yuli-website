What we look for

  • Deep subject expertise, especially in GCSE & A-Level subjects.
  • Clear, friendly communication with students and parents.
  • Firm commitment to safeguarding and inclusivity.
  • Reliability, punctuality, and professionalism.

How to apply

Send your CV and a short note to yuli.learning.uk@gmail.com. Include availability, subjects, and any teaching experience.
