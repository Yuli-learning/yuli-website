How we handle your data for private tutoring.

1

Who we are

This privacy notice explains how Yuli, operated by Lukas Jovaisa, processes personal data for private tutoring.

Contact: yuli.learning.uk@gmail.com

Jurisdiction: England and Wales

2

What data we collect

I collect the minimum data needed to deliver tutoring and communicate with you. This may include:

  • Contact details for the parent or student (name, email, phone)
  • Subject interests, availability, and learning goals
  • Session notes and shared materials
  • Payment and billing information (handled by payment providers)

I do not record sessions unless we both agree in writing.

3

How we use your data

I use your personal data to:

  • Provide tutoring services and manage bookings
  • Communicate about sessions and share resources
  • Process payments and issue invoices
  • Improve materials and lesson planning
  • Comply with legal obligations (tax, safeguarding)

I do not sell your data to third parties.

4

Lawful bases

Depending on the activity, I rely on:

  • Contract: To deliver tutoring services you request
  • Legitimate interests: Scheduling, basic analytics, improving materials
  • Legal obligation: Tax records, accounting, safeguarding
  • Consent: Marketing communications (you can withdraw anytime)
5

Cookies and similar technology

The website uses essential cookies for security and performance. If analytics are added in future, I will update this policy and provide an opt-out where required.

No tracking cookies are currently used without your consent.

6

Sharing your data

I may share data with trusted service providers strictly to provide the service:

  • Video conferencing platforms (for online sessions)
  • Cloud storage services (for lesson materials)
  • Email services (for communication)
  • Payment processors (for billing)

All providers are required to keep data secure and act only on my instructions.

7

International transfers

If a service provider stores data outside the UK or EEA, I ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as standard contractual clauses or adequacy decisions.

8

Data retention

I keep personal data only for as long as necessary:

  • Financial records: Up to 6 years (legal requirement)
  • Session materials: Up to 2 years after last session
  • Contact details: Until you request removal or service ends

Longer periods may be required by law in some cases.

9

Security

I take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal data, including:

  • Access controls and password protection
  • Encryption where appropriate
  • Regular security updates
  • Limiting access to authorised personnel only
10

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Erasure: Request deletion in certain circumstances
  • Restrict processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format

To exercise these rights, email yuli.learning.uk@gmail.com. You also have the right to complain to the ICO.

11

Children

For students under 18, a parent or guardian should handle bookings and communications. Safeguarding is a priority, and I may pause or refuse services if concerns arise.

See our Safeguarding policy for more information.

12

Changes to this policy

I may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be effective when posted on this page. The latest version will always be available here.
